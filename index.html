So lets get to bragging about myself! I went to the top ranked coding bootcamp here in San Diego (Origin Code Academy). I am a local software engineer with a background in Javascript, nodejs, express, mongo, (MEAN Stack), JQuery, C#, SQL, Bootstrap, .NET, ASP.NET, HTML5/CSS3, and among many other technologies. I have developed skills in software developing and tend to explore and learn the new technologies tentatively. My most recent project was a full stack document management system that allowed a more seamless interaction between the attorneys of the Mashiri Law Firm and their clients. In addition, I worked on my own portfolio for a side project with the use of HTML5,CSS3, Bootstrap, and Javascript.

Throughout my life, I’ve worked very hard in every field. While working hard, I also tend to learn very fast. In addition, I have natural leadership and therefore bring experienced leadership to the team. At Origin Code Academy, I was chosen to be the lead developer for our class project (Origin Lounge). A web based application where students and faculty can stay connected and up to date with current assignments and events. When I attended San Diego Mesa College, I was team captain of Mesa College’s soccer team. A team captain is chosen by the head coach to be the on-pitch leader of the team. It is often one of the members with experience and a player that can heavily influence a game.

Before I made the transition into software development, I worked at a law firm as an attorney’s assistant. The work I did was to manage cases, files, paperwork, and help with doing detailed research about the law and the case it was related to. Prior to working as an assistant, I worked at Wells Fargo Bank and was promoted within the company to lead and manage the employees at the company. At Wells Fargo I advanced my skills in being organized, on time, having great leadership with the team, and providing excellent customer service to clients.

I found software developing very interesting because my personality is the type that loves problem solving and putting the pieces of the puzzle together. I really stride in wanting to find a solution to a problem. Once I discovered coding, I knew that this is what I wanted to do for the rest of my life. I am a software developer that loves learning, passionate about coding, self-motivated about coding, and hungry to code.
